What the numbers mean for genai-api

  • Rust is 3-5x faster in CLI benchmarks, but most of Go's time is process startup (~25ms runtime init + tiktoken table load)
  • In genai-api, the compressor is loaded once as a library. The per-request overhead is sub-millisecond for typical inputs
  • LLM calls take 1-30 seconds. Even at 30ms compression overhead, it's <0.1% of total latency
  • Go gives us: single binary with genai-api, no Rust toolchain in CI, no FFI boundary, native goroutines for parallelism
